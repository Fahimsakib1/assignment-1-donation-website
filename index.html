Foods For Families

Donating to a homeless charity is always a good idea. And considering that most of them are run by non-profit organizations, they really do need those donations. Food is one of the most basic human needs and is also usually the most needed item at a homeless charity. But while fresh foods might be healthier and may seem like the more logical choice, they don’t last that long.

Shelter For Families

Not everyone gets to have a roof over their head every night or a bedroom all to themselves. For those of us who understand how fortunate we are, hearing that others go without things that we consider essentials is disconcerting at best and heart-wrenching at worst. When you feel the call to help those less fortunate, it’s understandable that you would want to know you’re helping as much as you can.

Education For Children

MORE than 21 million Bangladeshis live in extreme poverty and they cannot pay for the education of their children. A lack of education-friendly environment, ignorance of the parents and financial constraints often cause dropouts from the school. Eventually, it creates a vicious circle in society — being poor, they cannot afford education and as they cannot afford education, they remain poor.
